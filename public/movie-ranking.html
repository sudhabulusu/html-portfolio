The Best Movies According to Sudha

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Lovebirds

Issa Rae and Kumail Nanjiani together in this movie had me laughing out loud. I watched it twice.

The Shawshank Redemption

Great story and filmmaking taken to the next level by Morgan Freeman's narration and role as Red.

WALL-E

My favorite Pixar movie, definitely not just for little ones.
